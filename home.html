Self introduction

My name is Lingyi Zhu, or you can call me Leo, and I just graduated from the CS program at BU MET. I have many hobbies, such as photography and coffee. I started to get into boutique coffee in 2018. My good friend took me to many boutique coffee shops, and then I gradually started to learn about coffee. In the summer of 2019, I became a barista. Then after working for a while, I decided to continue studying. So I came to Boston University in 2020. Coincidentally, the SCA held a coffee show in Boston in 2020. The Specialty Coffee Association (SCA) is a trade association built on foundations of openness, inclusivity, and the power of shared knowledge. I had a lot of fun at the show.

Now back to my major. wo started learning something about front-end. I learned something about react and decided to take CS601 to learn more about web development. My undergraduate degree is in computer science from Auburn University. The more I learn about CS and all the possible career paths within it, the less sure I am about what I really want to do after graduation. I will be graduating at Dec 2023. Currently, I am working on becoming a full-stack developer, after mastering back-end development. I hope to learn more about front-end development from this course.
